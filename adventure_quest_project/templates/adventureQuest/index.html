Adventure Quest

About Us

Adventure Quest is an interactive treasure hunt game. It allows people to explore the city while also learning and having fun. Each quest has its own unique theme and will take you on a magical journey through the city. The quests are composed of multiple riddles and challenges. Players must solve each clue in order to be given the next riddle and location. This will allow players to discover the hidden gems away from the beaten path.

“The perfect combination of problem solving and the great outdoors” - The Guardian

About The Team

Adventure Quest was created at the University of Glasgow by students of MSc Software Development, as part of a project for the Internet Technology course. The students involved were Tom Esparon, Holly Maxwell Pringle, Kirsten Miller, Susie Smart and Hannah Pankow. The site is built using Python, HTML, CSS, Ajax and JavaScript and the aim was for us to create a site using the Django framework. Ideally this project would work best as an app and if given the opportunity to develop it, we think it could be quite successful.
